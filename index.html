ABOUT JMC

Meet Jamal Jawhar

Jamal Jawhar has been serving the Mid-South community since 1993 — over 30 years keeping families comfortable through Mississippi's brutal summers and cold winter nights. What started as a one-man operation built on word-of-mouth has grown into one of DeSoto County's most trusted HVAC names, with a loyal customer base that keeps coming back and referring friends.

Jamal's motto is simple: "One Call Does It All." Whether it's a gas fireplace repair, a full AC system replacement, or a routine tune-up before summer hits, he handles it personally — with the kind of care and communication that big HVAC companies just can't match.

He's an Angi Super Service Award winner (2025), holds a 4.6-star rating across 90+ HomeAdvisor reviews, and offers a 100% satisfaction guarantee with a warranty on all products installed.
